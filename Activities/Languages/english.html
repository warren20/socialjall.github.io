Overcoming language obstacles

Asylum seekers arriving in Hong Kong are from varous background and various social status. Some of them are well educated with fluency in English whiles others speak no other language than their mother tongue.

Face to that environment, some NGO took a step to help asylum seekers and refugees to learn English as it is useful not only to communicate about their cases but also to express their feeling.

When the course ends students receive certificates for their knowledge of the language.
